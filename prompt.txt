App Name
Server Watcher

Subtitle（30字以內）
Monitor servers & websites at a glance

Description（英文）
Keep an eye on all your servers and websites — right from your home screen.

Server Watcher lets you monitor any HTTP/HTTPS endpoint or TCP host in real time. Add your servers once and get instant status updates with response latency, so you always know what's up and what's down.

Key Features

HTTP & TCP monitoring — supports websites, APIs, and raw TCP hosts/ports
Live latency display — see response time color-coded green, orange, or red
Home Screen Widgets — Small, Medium, and Large widgets show your server status at a glance without opening the app
Configurable refresh interval — choose from 30 seconds to 30 minutes, or manual only
Export & Import — back up your server list as JSON; restore or merge across devices
20 languages supported — English, 繁體中文, 简体中文, 日本語, 한국어, Français, Deutsch, Español, Português, Italiano, Nederlands, Русский, العربية, ภาษาไทย, Tiếng Việt, Bahasa Indonesia, Polski, Türkçe, Українська, हिन्दी
